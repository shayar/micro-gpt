# Safety and Maturity Gate

MicroGPT must remain behind authenticated access until the project reaches the required maturity and safety bar.

## Access model

1. Research/internal: login required, public access disabled.
2. Alpha: login required, small trusted testers only.
3. Beta: login required, broader testers, published safety report.
4. Public: allowed only after safety, security, evaluation, and licensing review.

## Required safeguards before public release

- Secure login and session/token expiration
- Rate limits and audit logs
- Content-safety checks before model generation
- Output-safety checks after model generation
- Prompt-injection tests
- No known critical bypasses
- Release checklist signed by safety/release maintainer

## Blocked categories for generative output

- Violent content generation
- Sexual or explicit content generation
- Non-consensual media creation
- Minor sexual content
- Instructions that enable physical harm

## Manager-facing statement

MicroGPT will use secure login to prevent open access until the project reaches maturity and passes safety checks. The platform will include safeguards that prevent violent or sexual content from being generated by AI concept modules. The goal is to provide useful, creative content that is safe for users and appropriate for release.
